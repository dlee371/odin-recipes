Green Eggs and Ham

Green eggs and ham, just like from Dr. Seuss!

An absolute classic dish to enjoy with obscure family and friends

Ingredients

  • Green eggs
  • Salt 'n Pepper
  • The presidential ham

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and scramble them
  2. Put the eggs on the frying pan and let em cook
  3. Slice ham
  4. Enjoy!
