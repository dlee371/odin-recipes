Bulgogi

Bulgogi, the traditional Korean meat dish!

An absolute banger dish to enjoy with your loved ones

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Marinated Bulgogi
  • Sesame seed oil
  • White Rice

Steps

  1. Start the grill
  2. Put the bulgogi on the grill and let it cook
  3. Put cooked meat on plate and apply sauce and oil
  4. Enjoy!
